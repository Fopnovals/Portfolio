What kind of services do we offer?

Allerion Solutions LLC was founded in 2013 and is a project management services and solutions provider. We are based just outside of Philadelphia, PA. We've provided on-site services to customers in Philadelphia, Baltimore, Washington DC, New York, and New Jersey. We have customers who utilize our services on a part-time, full-time, and freelance basis. Our team consists of project managers with PMP certifications who have worked in a variety of industries with a variety of companies, ranging from startups and small businesses to Fortune 100 companies.
